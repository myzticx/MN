All about Konnectrr

Konnnectrr is a brand that connects manufacturers and designers/start-up brands using lists. Our aim is to make it easier for designers/start-up brands and manufacturers to find each other. Our platform is designed to help designers/start-up brands find the right manufacturers to produce their products. We understand that the process of finding the right manufacturer can be a daunting task, which is why we have created a simple and easy-to-use platform that connects designers/start-up brands and manufacturers. With Konnnectrr, designers/start-up brands can easily find the right manufacturer for their products, and manufacturers can easily find the right designers/start-up brands to work with. We are passionate about helping designers/start-up brands and manufacturers connect, and we are committed to making the process as simple and straightforward as possible.
